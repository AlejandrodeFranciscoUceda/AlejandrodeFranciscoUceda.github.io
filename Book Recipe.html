Recipe #1: Galician octopus

  • Time: 50 min
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Octopus 1 (of3 kg)
Onion 3
Bay leaf 1
Papricka -
Extra virgin olive oil -
Salt -

Preparation

  • Step 1: If the octopus is not clean, take the innards out of the head and clean it. Then, freeze it during 3 days for it to tenderize. Defrost it the previous night in the fridge.
  • Step 2: Boil in a big pot water with onion and the bay leaf. Introduce the octopus and leave it on a fast burn until it starts boiling again. Then, reduce the temperature and cover it.
  • Step 3: Boil it during 35 minutes approximately and after it leave it rest 15 minutes more in water.
  • Step 4: Drain it well, without letting it getting cold, and cut the tentacles in slices.

Recipe#2: Easy Meatloaf

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Ground beef 1 and 1/2 pounds
Egg 1
Onion 1
Milk 1 cup
Dried bread crumbs 1 cup
salt and pepper to taste 1
tablespoons brown sugar 2
tablespoons prepared mustard 2
Ketchup 1/3 cup

Preparation

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • Step 2: In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread OR cracker crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, or form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  • Step 3: In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  • Step 4: Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.

Recipe#3: Banana Banana Bread

  • Time: 80 min
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Baking soda 2 teacups
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Butter 1/2 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Beaten egg 2
Mashed overripe bananas 2 and 1/3 cups

Preparation

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  • Step 2: In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  • Step 3: Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.

Recipe#4: Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Quantity
Butter, softened 1 cup
White sugar 1 cup
Packed brown sugar 1 cup
Egg 2
Vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Hot water 2 teaspoons

Preparation

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • Step 2: Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  • Step 3: Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
